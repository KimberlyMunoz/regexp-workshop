Extracting Groups

Access paren’d groups programmatically.

Match a four-digit year, but extract the final two years.

Type: \d{2} ( \d{2} )

Hover mouse over a match. The first grouping (labeled $1) is the last two digits.

In an editor or language, change this: 

In 1941, the US retaliated and went to war with Japan.

To this: 

In the 1900's, the US retaliated and went to war with Japan.

Using this JavaScript code: 

s.replace(/(\d{2})\d{2}/,
          "the $100's")
