Groupings

Find year ranges.

Type: \d{4} - \d{2}

Both years and year ranges? Try or ...

Type: \d{4} - \d{2} | \d{4}

To make question mark work with multiple characters, group it with parens.

Type: \d{4} ( - \d{2} )?

+ and * work on groups too.

Searching for actual parens?
Escape them, via: \( and \)
